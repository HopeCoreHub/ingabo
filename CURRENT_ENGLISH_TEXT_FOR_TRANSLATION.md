## NOTES FOR TRANSLATION

1. **Privacy Policy**: All Privacy Policy related content should remain in English, French, and Kiswahili only. Do not translate to Kinyarwanda.

2. **AI Content Policy Notice**: This entire notice should remain in English, French, and Kiswahili only.

3. **Proper Nouns**: Keep proper nouns like "HopeCore Hub", "Mahoro", "Muganga", "Isange One Stop Center", "Rwanda Investigation Bureau (RIB)", "MTN Mobile Money", "Aline IRADUKUNDA" as they are.

4. **Phone Numbers**: Keep phone numbers as-is (e.g., "0780332779", "+250780332779", "3029", "3512").

5. **Emojis**: Keep emojis as-is (e.g., "üíú", "üè¥").

6. **Error Messages**: Translate the error message text, but keep technical error details in brackets [error] as placeholders.

7. **Date Formats**: Translate day and month names, but keep the format structure (e.g., "Mon 3, Nov" becomes translated day name, number, translated month name).

8. **Placeholders**: Text like "[username]", "[userId]", "[number]", "[error]" are placeholders and should not be translated.
